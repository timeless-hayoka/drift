# DRIFT — Investor Pitch

## The Problem

AI agents are everywhere. CrewAI, AutoGPT, LangChain — they can book flights, write code, research topics, answer customers. But they all share the same fatal flaw:

**They are soulless executors.**

- They run until they crash, with no sense of their own limits
- They drown in infinite context, with no selective attention
- They make the same mistakes repeatedly, with no self-reflection
- They feel no urgency, no fatigue, no curiosity — just prompt → response

When a customer service bot misses social cues, when a coding agent burns through tokens on a dead-end, when a research agent goes down a rabbit hole — the problem isn't the LLM. The problem is that the agent has **no interior life**.
